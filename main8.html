Mona Lisa

Artist: Leonardo da Vinci
Description: World’s most famous portrait, painted between 1503–1506.
Known for its enigmatic smile.
Once thought unfinished.

The Scream

Artist: Edvard Munch
Description: Expressionist masterpiece symbolizing anxiety and despair.
Painted in 1893.

The Great Wave

Artist: Katsushika Hokusai
Description: Famous Japanese woodblock print (1831).
Part of series: Thirty-six Views of Mount Fuji.

Girl with a Pearl Earring

Artist: Johannes Vermeer
Description: A young girl with a striking pearl earring, painted c.1665.
Sometimes called the Mona Lisa of the North.
